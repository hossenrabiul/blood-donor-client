Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our service.

1. What is the purpose of the web app?

This web app is designed to connect blood donors with individuals in need of blood donations. It allows users to create blood requests, donate blood, and search for available donors.

2. How do I create a blood request?

To create a blood request, log in to your account, navigate to the blood request section, and fill out the form with your details. Your request will be sent to all registered donors who match your blood type.

3. How can I donate blood?

To donate blood, log in to your account, navigate to the donation section, and express your willingness to donate. You can also search for blood requests in need of your blood type and volunteer.

4. Can I contact a donor directly?

Yes, you can contact any donor directly via the platform if you urgently need blood. The contact details of the donor will be available to you once they agree to donate.
